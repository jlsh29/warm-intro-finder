💤 Dormant bridges

Some of your strongest intro opportunities aren't with new people — they're with the 1st-degree contacts you've drifted away from. This page ranks your direct connections by how much 2nd-degree reach they unlock, weighted by how many months since you last talked. A high score means: "if you reconnect with this person, you reactivate a big slice of your network."

Score = 2nd-degree contacts unlocked × months since last interaction. Recompute every render from identities.csv.
