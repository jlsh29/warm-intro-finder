📘 How to Use Warm Intro Finder

← Back to finder

What is Warm Intro Finder?

Warm Intro Finder helps you discover the shortest, strongest route through your network to reach anyone — by chaining together mutual connections on Twitter, Farcaster, LinkedIn, and on-chain wallets. Instead of guessing who knows whom, it shows you the path, the strength of each hop, and drafts the outreach message for you.

Why use it? Cold messages get ignored. A warm intro — "hey, we both know X" — gets a reply 3-5× more often. This tool turns every social graph you already have into a pathfinding problem and hands you the answer.

1

Set up your profile

Click Edit profile at the top of the home page and fill in your name + the handles you use on each platform: Twitter, Farcaster, LinkedIn, DeBank wallet. You don't need all four — the more you add, the more platforms the finder can search.

Example: Name "Jen Lim", Twitter "@jenlim", LinkedIn "jen-lim", and a DeBank wallet. Save and the app generates a synthetic 3-degree network around you.
2

How the system builds your network

On save, the app seeds your 1st, 2nd, and 3rd-degree contacts from the handles you provided. Each connectionConnection (edge)A typed relationship between two people — direct, mutual, shared-org, or platform similarity. Each has a strength score. records the platforms both sides share and their interaction volume.

You can visualize it live on the Network graphNetwork graphInteractive view of your full network. Your node is the diamond at the center; the highlighted path is the one the finder picked. panel — the diamond is you, and edge color reflects strength.

3

How to search for a target

Type the person's name, handle, or wallet into Find target. Autocomplete shows every reachable person with their degree from you. Click the match, or type freely and press Find warm path.

Example: Search "Sofia" or "@pascalokafor" or even part of a wallet. If Sofia is 2nd-degree, the finder will route through the strongest mutual.
4

Understanding the 3 ranked paths

The finder returns up to 3 paths ranked by a composite scoreComposite scoreWeighted ranking: 40% fewer-hops, 35% more mutual platforms, 25% stronger edges. Higher = better path.:

  • 🥇 Best Path — highest composite score overall
  • 🥈 Good Path — second-best alternative
  • 🥉 Possible Path — a backup if the first two don't work

Each path shows every hop as a card with the person's handle, platforms, and interaction chips. The arrow between hops is colored by strength.

5

How to read interaction scores and strength badges

Each person has a interaction score 0-15Interaction scoreAggregated from likes, comments, reposts, recasts, endorsements, and on-chain txs. 0-15 scale, higher means more active with you.. Each edge between two people has a strength bucket:

🔥 Very Strong 12-15 💪 Strong 8-11 👋 Moderate 4-7 🤝 Weak 1-3

Every node also has an Influence badgeInfluence (0-100)Composite of connections count, average interaction score, and platform coverage. Higher influence = bigger multiplier if they say yes to introducing you. in the top-right showing how well-connected they are (0-100).

6

How to use the outreach features

Click Select this path on the path card you want, then expand the outreach section below. You get:

⏰ Best Time to Reach OutSuggests the best day/time to message this person based on their interaction history.
📤 Send Warm Intro MessageExpands a per-platform tab with a pre-drafted message referencing your mutual connection.
✨ Generate with AIUses Claude to rewrite the message in a warmer, more personalized tone. Edit before sending.
💬 Send via Warm Intro FinderQueues an in-system message (for when social logins are activated).
📋 CopyCopies the message to clipboard so you can paste it into the platform yourself.
🔗 Open PlatformOpens the target's profile / DM compose page on that platform.
Tip: Use 🔀 Compare Paths (bottom of results) to see the 3 paths side-by-side with risk levels and backup platforms.
7

How to track your outreach success

The Warm Intro ProgressWarm Intro ProgressA 4-stage pipeline that auto-advances as you use the outreach buttons. Persisted per target. bar at the top of every outreach card has four stages:

  • 1. Message Sent — triggers automatically when you click Send Warm Intro Message or submit the in-system composer
  • 2. Awaiting Reply — triggers automatically when you click Mark as Sent on a platform tab
  • 3. Meeting Scheduled — click this stage yourself once the reply turns into a meeting
  • 4. Goal Achieved — click when the intro delivers the outcome you wanted

Your aggregate reply, meeting, and success rates show up in the Total Outreach tile-row right under the stats dashboard on the home page.

8

How to read the weekly report

Click 📊 View Weekly Report in the navbar to open /report. Hover any metric label (the ? icon) for an inline explanation. The report covers:

  • Outreach summary — reply / meeting / success rates this week
  • Network activity — bar chart of interactions per day over the last 7 days
  • New this week — fresh contacts to follow up on while the signal is warm
  • Strongest new relationships — the highest-strength edges that touch this week's new contacts
  • Top 3 to reach out to this week — un-contacted high-influence, high-score people
Export: Use your browser's Print dialog (or the 🖨 button) — the report has a print stylesheet, so "Save as PDF" gives you a clean one-page PDF.
